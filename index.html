Bakery

About Us

When in New Paltz, don't miss a trip to The Bakery. Residents consider The
Bakery, with its rustic outdoor cafe and beautiful gardens, to be the center of
social life in New Paltz, a place to meet people, bump into old friends, or sit
quietly and read the papers. Known since 1981 for great bagels, croissants
rolls, rugulah, danish and butter cookies, The Bakery includes a coffee bar and
full lunch menu. Experience New Paltz! Visit The Bakery.
